About

This is a Navajo verb morphology parser. It takes a Navajo verb and determines its internal structure based on Navajo verb morphology/phonology rules. I did this work in conjunction with Dr. Aryeh Faltz at Arizona State University as the final year project of my undergraduate studies.

See the Help page if you're having difficulty using the parser.
